What is The Eden Collective?

A farm.

Our community farm is a champion of the urban farming movement bringing sustainability and resilience back into the heart of city life.

A journal.

More than just a journal, this is the vanguard of the intellectual revival pondering the age-old question: what does it mean to live the good life?

A community.

We believe the good life, either with dirt under your fingernails or inkstains on your hands, is one meant to be shared.

Farm happenings

Monthly farm open days

We host a farmer's market, pizza night, craft beers and live music, every third Saturday of the month.

Volunteer with us

Whether you're a retiree looking for a hobby or an office worker seeking escape from the corporate grind, our community farm has a place for you.

Fresh produce, delivered

Curate your own box of fresh and seasonal produce and we'll have it delivered to your door.
